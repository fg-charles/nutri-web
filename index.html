• Nutrition Cycle Calculator

Do you eat the same thing every n number of days? If so, this tool will let you find your average daily intake of nutrients and calories. This way, you can check your diet for deficiencies and imbalances and adjust accordingly!

Instructions:

  1. Enter the length of your nutrition cycle (in days) (default 1)
  2. Add all the ingredients you eat within your cycle and provide nutrition information. If left empty, a category will default to zero.
  3. Click on "Get Nutrition Report".
